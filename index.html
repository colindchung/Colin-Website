Overview

Hey! My name is Colin and this is website is dedicated to my personal projects. My projects range from web development to IoT circuits to hand-crafted wooden puzzles. I was first introduced to programming when I was in the 7th grade when I joined a First Robotics team. Since then, code has intrigued me and I have taken many computer science courses and worked several types of tasks for tech companies. Through my experience, I have developed several projects in many different programming languages. I also picked up Arduino and woodworking in middle school which continue to spark my interest. I created this website to showcase my learning over the years and my passion for designing and writing interesting products.



Hobbies and Interests

Since most of the skills i need for school and employment are intellectual, I enjoy hobbies such as woodworking, circuits, sports and activities where I use my hands and body. What Am I Learning?

There's so much to learn in tech, so where do I even start? I am becoming comfortable with Object Oriented languages, and I feel like I can learn new language syntax relatively quickly. My main focus in the near future is to master some IoT and hardware design languages, as well as learn more about the back-end. Luckily for me, I am enrolled in several circuits courses, including labs where I am programming an FPGA with VDHL and Verilog. I also enjoy using Arduino as a platform for simple projects. As for the back-end, I'd like to master the art of building and using APIs, creating stored procs, and using different database technologies and platforms (MongoDB, PostgreSQL, NoSQL, Oracle). Hopefully by the time I graduate, I'll have gained plenty of experience from my elective courses, co-op terms and personal projects!
